My favourite foods

A hamburger, often known as a burger, consists of fillings—usually a patty of panfried or grilled ground meat, typically beef—placed inside a sliced bun, sesame seed bun, or bread roll.These patties are often served with lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, bacon, or chilis, together in the bun or roll.



Rajma Chawal is a popular North Indian dish featuring spiced, thick red kidney bean curry (Rajma) served with steamed Basmati rice. It is made by boiling soaked kidney beans, then cooking them in a onion-tomato-ginger-garlic masala base. This creamy dish is a classic lunch staple often served with onions and cumin-topped rice.
